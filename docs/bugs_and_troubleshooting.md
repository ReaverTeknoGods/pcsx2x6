# Bugs and troubleshooting

You have an issue/bug with PCSX2x6?

you may:
- [open an issue ticket at github](https://github.com/PS2Homebrew-arcade/pcsx2x6/issues/new/choose)
- Join the [PCSX2x6 discord server](https://discord.gg/7fBbpRZMm)


!!! note "Graphical issues"

    Keep in mind the developer of this fork can't help much with graphical issues, you may try asking for help on official PCSX2 channels/github/forums.
    but be warned they hate everything arcade related and you may be banned from wherever you ask
